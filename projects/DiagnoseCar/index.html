Diagnose Car Mixed Reality

Summary

Diagnose Car is an initiative of Connectief, in collaboration with Febiac, Since cars are becoming increasingly complex in terms of electronics, it is not always easy to inform students on a technology that changes more regularly than before and on the other hand to allow them to make mistakes and learn using a technology that is also becoming increasingly expensive and fragile. Diagnosis Car looks for the solution in Augmented & Mixed Reality. With the Hololens we offer them a solution with which students can visualize the cars in 3D, and with which they can also learn the procedures in mixed reality. The application will also be built for smartphones and tablets.
